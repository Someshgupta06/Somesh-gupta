ZEDLOX POWER CHUCK

ZEDLOX - 3 Jaw Open Centre standard Power Chucks rotate at high speeds on CNC Turning. These are available in Ø135mm, Ø 170mm, Ø 210mm, Ø 250mm, Ø 315mm. We supply standard soft jaws. We offer serration: 1.5 x 60, 2.5 x 60, 3 x 60 on base jaws, hard jaws and soft jaws Available.

2 jaws chuck

2 jaws have two jaws facing each other and are suitable for holding irregularly shaped for holding work pieces but the gripping force is high

3 jaws chuck

3 jaws chuck are versatile and self centring typically used for round or hexagonal workpieces in general machining tasks

4 jaws chuck

4 jaws chucks province impendent adjustments offering greater flexibility for irregularly shaped or asymmetrical workpieces
